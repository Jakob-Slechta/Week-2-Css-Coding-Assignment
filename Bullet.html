Blazblue's Bullet, she's mostly a side character in the grand scheme of things but she's very likeable. A young orphan girl raised by mercanaries, the only thing she really understands is combat. So she's almost always having her guard up, there's never really a moment of rest in her eyes. She's downright anti-social as a result of always being in combat mood, which pushes others away from her. Little do they know, she's as kind as a person can come.



Once again, I'm left to wonder how Bullet got those cowlicks she calls hair...
